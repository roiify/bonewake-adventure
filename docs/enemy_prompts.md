# Enemy Sprite Prompts — Zombie Variants

Ready-to-paste PixelLab prompts for new zombie enemies. Each ends with the BoneWake house suffix so they slot in with the existing roster aesthetically.

## House suffix (append to every prompt)

```
pixel art, side-view east-facing full body, gritty mature dark fantasy gory survival horror, physical decay (exposed bone, peeling flesh, sunken eye sockets), active dripping blood, weathered tattered gear, dark muted palette with deep crimson accents
```

---

## 1. bloated_glutton — pus-filled tank, explodes on death

```
Grossly bloated zombie with stretched yellow-green skin straining against burst stitches, distended belly leaking dark pus, fat-laden chains digging into folds of rotted flesh, broken teeth oozing bile, swollen veins glowing sickly red
```

## 2. crawling_torso — fast, low-HP creeper

```
Limbless zombie torso dragging itself forward with two skeletal arms, trailing entrails and a smear of black blood, jaw distended in a permanent scream, raw spinal column whipping behind it like a tail
```

## 3. hangman — chain-swinging executioner zombie

```
Tall hooded zombie executioner with a frayed noose still around his snapped neck, head tilted at a broken angle, swinging a heavy iron chain wrapped in dried gore, wearing a moth-eaten black hood and a blood-crusted leather apron
```

## 4. plague_doctor_undead — debuff applier with syringe

```
Zombified medieval plague doctor with a cracked beaked mask leaking dark fluid, broken glass lenses revealing rotted eye sockets, tattered waxed coat dripping with congealed blood, wielding a rust-pitted bone-saw and a syringe filled with black ichor
```

## 5. conjoined_horror — two-headed mini-boss

```
Massive two-headed zombie fused at the shoulders, both heads decomposed at different rates (one fresh and screaming, one skeletal), four mismatched arms wielding a cleaver and a femur club, stitched torso splitting open to show ribs
```

## 6. maggot_host — summons swarm on hit

```
Emaciated zombie whose stomach has split open into a writhing nest of white maggots, jaw hanging loose with grubs crawling out, hollow ribcage hosting black flies, fingers worn down to wet bone
```

## 7. stitched_brute — Frankenstein-style boss

```
Towering brute stitched together from many corpses, mismatched skin tones poorly sewn with thick black sutures, iron rivets bolted into his skull, one arm massive and gorilla-like the other shriveled, swinging a butcher's maul slick with brain matter
```

## 8. drowned_lurker — swamp variant

Bloated, water-logged zombie covered in green-black river slime and rotted seaweed, gray puffy skin sloughing off in sheets, mouth and lungs gushing brown water, tangled fishing net wrapped around its waist with rusted hooks dangling

## 10. plague_bride — ceremonial slow-walking elite

# Endgame Bosses

## 13. alpha_voidlord — cosmic endgame boss (final chapter)

Reference: cosmic warlord figure haloed by eight glowing orbs beneath a runic "Alpha" sigil, wielding a chain-bound lightning lance.

**Override the house suffix for this one** — it's a cosmic boss, not a rotting zombie. Use this suffix instead:

```
pixel art, side-view east-facing full body, epic dark fantasy cosmic horror, deep violet and indigo palette with electric cyan highlights, glowing arcane runes, starfield backdrop, painterly detail with crisp pixel edges
```

Prompt:

```
Towering void-armored archlord floating above the battlefield, ornate galaxy-textured plate mail swirling with nebula clouds and constellations, void-black hooded mantle trailing wisps of purple flame and ribbon-banners inscribed with glowing runes, single piercing white eye burning from the shadow of his hood, halo of eight floating orbs of pure white starlight orbiting his head beneath an alpha-sigil sun-disc of runic gold, gauntleted hand gripping a massive crystalline greatsword crackling with arcs of violet lightning and wrapped in rattling iron chains, a smaller horned demonic familiar tethered at his hip glaring with twin blue eyes, weightless cape billowing in a cosmic wind
```
